Eddie Liu's Echoes in the Multiverse Series

This series explores alternate timelines and catastrophic convergences through the eyes of Eddie Liu, a key figure in You Cannot Have the Stars. Each story offers a glimpse into a universe on the edge of annihilation—or salvation.

  • Echoes of the Assembly
    Labor leader Tabitha Bosanac joins a fierce workers' revolt against entrenched corporate tyranny in a desperate bid for justice and dignity.
  • Echoes of the Vanished
    Sebastian Adebayo struggles to survive in a world where 70% of the population mysteriously disappears, reshaping society in unexpected ways.
  • Echoes of the Lost
    Tim Hall becomes a stowaway on a desperate colony ship after Earth is destroyed by a rogue black hole, seeking hope among the stars.
  • Echoes of the Unseen
    Eddie Liu and Zahra Hassan race against time to stop a secret parasitic alien invasion threatening humanity’s very existence.
  • Echoes of the Contagion
    Dani Harper and Elizabeth Serrano fight to survive and contain a deadly man-made virus that could topple society itself.
