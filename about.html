About You Cannot Have the Stars

You Cannot Have the Stars is the debut novel of Shawn Lyons, an author fascinated by the intersection of history, politics, and speculative futures. The novel grew from a question: What if one pivotal moment in history had changed the entire course of human society?

Set against a backdrop of political upheaval, personal trauma, and alien contact, the book explores how individuals and societies confront change, challenge ideology, and struggle with power. Shawn's vision imagines a world where history diverges dramatically yet retains a core humanity, asking how hope and fear coexist when everything we thought stable is uprooted.

The novel’s alternate timeline begins in 1920, when Eugene V. Debs is elected U.S. president, setting in motion a century-long transformation of America into a socialist superpower. This history reshapes politics, technology, culture, and the environment — leading to breakthroughs as well as fractures that test the resilience of its people.

At the heart of the narrative is an ensemble of eight deeply drawn characters, whose lives intersect across continents and crises. Their stories give voice to conflicting ideals and complex emotions — from hope to despair, loyalty to betrayal, and courage to doubt.

The Website

This site is designed as an immersive companion to the novel, featuring:

  • Fictional news reports and op-eds that broaden the world beyond the novel’s pages.
  • University lectures, government memos, and educational materials that deepen historical and scientific context.
  • Character speeches and private writings revealing inner thoughts and hidden agendas.
  • Glossary and background pages to help readers navigate the novel’s unique vocabulary and alternate history.

Every page, document, and excerpt is crafted to keep you fully inside the story’s universe — building a richer understanding of the stakes, tensions, and humanity at play.

About the Author

Shawn Lyons is an amateur author passionate about crafting layered stories that merge political complexity with speculative fiction. Inspired by real-world history and current global challenges, Shawn writes to explore what might have been and what still could be.

When not writing, Shawn enjoys reading widely, engaging with beta readers, and exploring new narrative forms to push storytelling boundaries. This website is an evolving project to support the novel’s world and connect with readers interested in alternate histories and thoughtful fiction.
